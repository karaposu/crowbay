# Legal/Platform Currency Check — 2026-06-12

## Summary (as reported)

Route 4 of the ToS-posture route-map — the legal currency verification — is
done, with better news than expected:

- **All six legal/platform claims CONFIRMED with verbatim current anchors.**
  The load-bearing one: 16 CFR §465.1 defines "fake indicators of social
  media influence" as those from "bots, purported individual accounts not
  associated with a real individual… hijacked accounts, or that otherwise do
  not reflect a real individual's or entity's activities, opinions, findings,
  or experiences" — a paid real-human like reflects a real activity, so the
  engagement row (allow+disclose) is textually sound. The catch-all's
  "opinions" prong is preserved as a counsel question.

  "expressly or by implication" on sentiment is a violation — which is
  precisely why "honest review, any stars" framing can't cure a paid public
  review.

  Maps review policy in April 2026 and now actively asks users whether

  live — the reviews gate ages in exactly the direction the finding

- **FTC enforcement is escalating** (late-2025 warning letters, penalties now
  $53,088/violation) — the gate-side conservatism is earning its keep.
